Photography Reflection

Shared Spaces, Separate Journeys

I chose to photograph this scene because I often find myself reflecting on how, at Hunter, there are so many of us moving through the same spaces, yet each of us is here for a completely different reason. Some of us chose this college out of a deep personal desire, while others arrived here because it was the opportunity that made the most sense at the time.

No matter the path that brought us here, we now share this environment. We are united by location and circumstance, yet divided by our individual goals, histories, and aspirations.

I think this photograph captures that idea clearly. The multiple figures crossing the sky bridge appear connected simply by occupying the same structure, but each person carries their own story, responsibilities, and dreams. Even though they are physically together in one place, their internal worlds are entirely separate.

To me, this image reflects the quiet complexity of campus life — a shared journey unfolding through countless individual experiences happening all at once.
